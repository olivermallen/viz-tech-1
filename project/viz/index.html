How can we visualize this with data from Tumblr?

This plot includes posts tagged #tradwife, #tradfem, or #tradblr collected from 2019-2022. These tags are tags often used by tradwife posters on tumblr, a popular microblogging site where Barbeau et. al. identified the Cottagecore to Tradwife Pipeline.

The network on the left shows which tags are being used by these tradwife blogs. Two tags are connected if they are often posted together, and the larger tags are posted more often. You can see the “landscape” of topics that tradwife blogs post about in this legend. By clicking on a tag you can see how often this tag appears in tradwife posts over time. Here we can see that “cottagecore” was peaked in April 2022, so Tumblr users searching the cottagecore tag at this time may have been exposed to tradwife content or accidentally amplified tradwife views.

Try clicking on other nodes to see if you can discover other trends. You can click on multiple tags at once, and you can de-select a tag by clicking on it.
